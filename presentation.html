The Challenge

Why This Platform Was Needed

The community faced real coordination problems that made collective development difficult, slow and untransparent.

Disconnected Diaspora

Members in France, Germany, UK and USA had no reliable way to stay connected to village decisions or contribute meaningfully.

No Financial Transparency

Donations were collected informally with no public tracking, creating distrust among potential donors abroad.

No Payment Infrastructure

No secure, structured way to accept donations from abroad — MoMo, PayPal and cards were not integrated.

No Governance Tools

Elections, petitions and community polls happened informally with no secure, verifiable process.

Dying Cultural Heritage

The Ngiemboon language and cultural traditions were undocumented, especially for younger diaspora generations.

Scattered Communication

News, events and updates were shared via WhatsApp groups and word of mouth — inconsistent and unreliable.

Democratic Governance

Elections, Polls & Petitions

Every registered member has a voice. The platform provides secure, transparent democratic tools for the whole community.

Elections

Candidate profiles with photos, manifestos and bios. Swipeable carousel voting with live leaderboard. One vote per registered member, results published instantly.

Community Polls

Admins create polls on any village decision. Members vote in their portal. Results shown in real time with percentage breakdowns.

Petitions

Community members launch petitions with goals and deadlines. Circular progress rings show signature progress. Signers celebrated with confetti animation.

Transparency Reports

Full financial reports and budget breakdowns published publicly. Dedicated Transparency and Budget pages accessible to all.

Governance Page

Organisation structure, leadership roles, bylaws and constitutional documents all accessible in one place.

Urgent Notices

Priority announcements appear as dismissable banners on the homepage. Members also receive push notifications in real time.

Architecture

  • REST API with JWT authentication and role-based access (admin / leader / member)
  • Password hashing with bcrypt, secure token rotation
  • Media via Cloudinary CDN — optimised images with auto-format
  • Video meetings via Daily.co — host privileges for admin/leader roles
  • Service worker for push notifications — works on mobile browsers
  • PostgreSQL with Prisma — typed schema, migrations, relations
Security
  • All routes protected with middleware authentication
  • Role checks on every sensitive endpoint
  • Input validation and sanitisation throughout

What's Next

Deployment & Launch Plan

The platform is complete and tested. Deployment is the next step.

STEP 1
Backend Deployment

Deploy Node.js API to Railway / Render. Connect PostgreSQL database. Set environment variables.

STEP 2
Frontend Deployment

Deploy React app to Vercel or Netlify. Configure API URL. Enable HTTPS.

STEP 3
Domain & DNS

Connect custom domain (e.g. nkenkak-ngiesang.com). Update DNS records. SSL certificate auto-issued.

STEP 4
Seed Data & Admin Setup

Create first admin account. Add initial projects, team members and content. Test all payment flows.

STEP 5
Launch & Onboard Members

Share registration link. Send welcome email campaign. Monitor and support first users.
